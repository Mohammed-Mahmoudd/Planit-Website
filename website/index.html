Who are we?

We are a team consisting of a group of students. We established our team in 2024, which is Codes-Ancestors. Our goal was to help students acquire a lot of information , get new skills and arranged there tasks with AI that will help them to be more efficient. The means of communication helped us in this, as there are many specialists in explaining a lot of information in many different fields, but not all users can arrange and prepare their tasks. Therefore, we help users in easily accessing this information and help them to be more efficient in their tasks.
